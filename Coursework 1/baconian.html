one of the more advance out of the three is the Baconian cipher,

The Baconian cipher or "bacons cipher" is a method of steganography created by Francis Bacon

here a message is concealed in the presentation of text as opposed to its content.

The cipher uses binary encoding - each letter is replaced by a group of five of the letters "A" or "B"
